# LockWitness Play Store Listing Draft

This draft is for Play Store review preparation and must be reviewed before submission.

## App Name

LockWitness

## Short Description

Owner-controlled failed unlock evidence recorder with local history and export.

## Full Description

LockWitness helps Android device owners record evidence when someone fails to unlock their own device. The app is local-first, permission-based, and built around clear user controls.

With LockWitness, the device owner can:

- Enable failed unlock monitoring with Device Admin activation.
- Keep a local timeline of failed unlock incidents.
- Optionally capture front-camera photo evidence.
- Optionally capture short front-camera video evidence with no audio.
- Optionally record location snapshot metadata.
- Review incident details, module statuses, paths, hashes, and device metadata.
- Export local evidence packages through explicit user action.
- Use user-initiated Android chooser flows for sharing local export packages.

LockWitness emphasizes transparent controls:

- Major evidence modules can be enabled or disabled independently.
- Camera and location use require Android runtime permissions.
- Export and share actions are initiated by the user.
- Evidence is stored locally on the device.
- Captured evidence is not silently transmitted.

## Content Notes

LockWitness is intended for use by the owner of the device where it is installed. The listing, screenshots, and in-app copy should show the app dashboard, settings toggles, permission explanations, diagnostics, history, detail, and export flow.

## Screenshot Plan

Recommended screenshots:

- Dashboard with current monitoring status.
- Settings showing independent toggles.
- Device Admin permission explanation.
- Diagnostics checklist.
- History timeline with sample local incident records.
- Incident detail showing statuses and hashes.
- Export action with user-initiated chooser flow.

Use sample data that is clearly synthetic and does not identify a real person.
